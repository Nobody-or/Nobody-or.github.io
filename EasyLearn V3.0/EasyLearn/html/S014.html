Buildings

Description

Xiao Zhang conducted a social survey during the summer vacation. The survey is to study how the color of a building affects people's mood. he found a community with buildings in a row from left to right.

There are n buildings in this community, and each building has a color ci and a height hi. The content of Xiao Zhang's investigation is that every time he stands between the i-th and (i+1)-th building and looks to the left, he records the number of builds’ colors he sees as the result of his investigation.

Since Xiao Zhang was obsessed with games during the summer vacation and did not have time to conduct field investigations, he must ask you to tell him the results of the investigations.

Input

There are multiple groups of data.
An integer n in the first row of each group of data. It means that there are n buildings in a row from left to right. n numbers in the second line represent the color of each building (1≤ci≤106). n numbers in the third row indicate the height of each building (1≤hi≤109).

Output

Each group of data outputs n numbers, the i-th number represents the number of colors of buildings that he can see when he stands between the i-th building and the (i+1)-th building and looks to the left.

Sample Input

2
5
1 2 3 4 5
1 3 4 2 5
5
1 2 4 4 5
1 3 4 2 5

Sample Output

1 1 1 2 1
1 1 1 1 1
